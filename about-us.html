The Roots of Rooting Research

As a young student at PGIMER, Chandigarh, I was always deeply intrigued by the complex interactions between microorganisms and human health. My passion for medical microbiology grew with every lecture and every experiment, and I realized that the path to understanding and combating diseases requires more than just theoretical knowledge—it requires an unyielding commitment to evidence, innovation, and practical applications.

Over the years, my research journey expanded beyond microbiology into areas like antimicrobial resistance, gastroenterology, hepatology, orthopedics, and health economics. Through my work, I witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by healthcare professionals and researchers alike—challenges such as the disconnect between scientific research and real-world healthcare implementation. This gap was all too evident in the way medical literature was reviewed, applied, and communicated. I also saw a need for more systematic, targeted research that would contribute not just to scientific knowledge but to tangible improvements in clinical practices and healthcare policies.

Having worked across hospitals, academia, and the pharmaceutical industry—including at Parexel International and Quantify Research—I began to understand that bridging this divide was crucial. I realized that there was a need for a research firm that could provide comprehensive and nuanced insights that would translate into better healthcare solutions. And that's how Rooting Research was born.

Why "Rooting Research"?
The name Rooting Research reflects my belief in the power of grounded, thorough research. The word “rooting” symbolizes a deep, foundational understanding—much like how a plant grows from its roots, robust research needs to be rooted in evidence and reality. I wanted to create a space where research would not be just theoretical, but would connect directly to the real-world challenges and solutions in healthcare.

At Rooting Research, we believe in digging deep to uncover the truths that matter, supporting evidence-based medicine, and providing the kind of targeted insights that drive change. Our approach ensures that every piece of research we do is built on strong, reliable foundations that can be translated into meaningful impact.

The Mission of Rooting Research
At Rooting Research, our mission is to bridge the gap between scientific research and its real-world applications in healthcare. We aim to provide rigorous, evidence-based research across various therapeutic areas that directly influences clinical practices, public health policies, and patient outcomes. We strive to:

  • Drive Innovation: By focusing on high-impact, high-quality research, we aim to contribute to groundbreaking developments in healthcare that improve lives.
  • Support Healthcare Professionals and Policymakers: We want to equip clinicians, researchers, and policymakers with the knowledge and evidence they need to make informed decisions.
  • Foster Collaboration: Our experience working in both the academic and industry sectors has taught us that collaboration is key to meaningful change. Through partnerships and collaboration, we can push the boundaries of what’s possible in healthcare.
  • Educate and Empower: We are committed to educating the next generation of healthcare researchers, scientists, and leaders, helping them build the skills to navigate the complex landscape of modern healthcare.

Ultimately, Rooting Research aims to be a transformative force in healthcare—ensuring that the knowledge we generate is not just published in journals but applied in ways that truly improve lives. From clinical settings to healthcare policy, we are determined to ensure that research becomes a catalyst for positive change in the world of medicine.
