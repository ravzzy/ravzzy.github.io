Dr. Anshul Sood

Founder / Director

Dr. Anshul Sood, founder of the company, holds a PhD in Medical Microbiology from PGIMER, Chandigarh, one of India's premier medical institutes. With over a decade of experience in healthcare, she has made significant contributions to anaerobic bacteriology, antimicrobial resistance, and evidence-based medicine. Dr. Sood's expertise spans systematic and targeted literature reviews across diverse therapeutic areas such as Gastroenterology, Hepatology, Orthopedics, Health Economics, and more.

She has authored 14+ first-author publications in leading journals, including Springer Nature and Elsevier, and served as a peer reviewer for esteemed scientific journals. Dr. Sood's research acumen includes successful grant proposal writing, contributing to a project that secured funding from the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR). She is also certified in Basic Health Economics, Research Methodology, and Biostatistics from PGIMER.Having worked in hospitals, academia, and industry—including Parexel International and Quantify Research—Dr. Sood brings a comprehensive understanding of research design, analysis, and communication. This expertise allows her to bridge the gap between scientific research, clinical practice, and healthcare policy, ensuring impactful outcomes across the sector.
