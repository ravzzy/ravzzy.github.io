Meet Our Founder

Dr. Anshul Sood

"Our mission has always been to bridge the gap between technology and human needs, creating solutions that make a real difference in people's lives."

Dr. Anshul Sood, founder of the company, holds a PhD in Medical Microbiology from PGIMER, Chandigarh, one of India's premier medical institutes. With over a decade of experience in healthcare, she has made significant contributions to anaerobic bacteriology, antimicrobial resistance, and evidence-based medicine. Dr. Sood's expertise spans systematic and targeted literature reviews across diverse therapeutic areas such as Gastroenterology, Hepatology, Orthopedics, Health Economics, and more.

She has authored 14+ first-author publications in leading journals, including Springer Nature and Elsevier, and served as a peer reviewer for esteemed scientific journals
